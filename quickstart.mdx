## Get Started with WISEPIM in 4 Simple Steps

Transform your product catalog and boost conversions in minutes. Follow this step-by-step guide to experience the power of AI-driven product information management.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Platform">
    [Set up your integration](/integrations/supported-platforms) with your e-commerce platform (Magento 2, WooCommerce, Shopify, Lightspeed, or CCV Shop).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import Your Products">
    [Import your products](/essentials/import-products) from your connected platform into WISEPIM for enrichment and optimization.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enrich with AI">
    [Use AI to enrich your products](/essentials/enrich-products) with compelling descriptions, optimized titles, and detailed attributes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export Back to Your Store">
    [Export your enhanced products](/essentials/export-products) back to your e-commerce platform and watch your conversions improve.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Before You Begin

### Prerequisites

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="store" title="E-commerce Platform Access">
    Ensure you have admin access to your e-commerce platform and API credentials ready. We support:
    - Magento 2 (API tokens)
    - WooCommerce (REST API keys)
    - Shopify (Private app credentials)
    - Lightspeed (API keys)
    - CCV Shop (API access)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="key" title="OpenAI API Key (Optional)">
    For AI enrichment features, you'll need an OpenAI API key. Get yours at [OpenAI Platform](https://platform.openai.com/account/api-keys).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### First-Time Setup

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="project-diagram" title="Create Your First Project">
    Projects help organize your product catalogs. Create a project for each brand, store, or product line you manage.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="settings" title="Configure Attributes">
    Set up custom product attributes that match your business needs. WISEPIM supports text, numbers, dates, selections, and more.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="wand-magic-sparkles" title="Set Up AI Prompts">
    Create reusable prompt templates for consistent, brand-aligned content generation across all your products.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
